Here is the challenge

Choose a website you are familiar with and find one functionality of that website
that you want to analyze. One small piece of it, such as commenting on a post on a news site.
You could also pick searching for a product on an ecommerce site,
or liking a post on a social media site. Once you have selected the website and the functionality,
create a persona with a user story that can be used to develop a use case/interaction flow
that is uniform with strategic design guidelines.

Persona

  • Name: Sarah
  • Age: 27
  • Occupation: Freelance writer
  • Location: New York City
  • Interests: Yoga, reading, cycling, hiking

    • Personal philosophy:
    Live simply and intentionally, focus on experiences rather than possessions.

User Story

As a freelance writer, Sarah has been actively using LinkedIn to connect with potential clients,
building her professional network.

Use Case

Sarah is a young professional living in a small studio apartment in New York City. She is a minimalist
who owns only the essentials and avoids clutter at all costs. Her clothing consists of a few high-quality
pieces that she can mix and match. She doesn't own a car and prefers to bike or walk everywhere.
Her apartment is sparsely furnished but filled with natural light and plants.
Sarah spends most of her free time practicing yoga, reading books, cycling, hiking, or exploring the city.
She prioritizes experiences over material possessions and values personal growth and self-improvement.
She is intentional about her purchases and only buys things that add value to her life.
Despite owning very little, Sarah feels content and fulfilled. She believes that living simply allows her to
focus on the things that matter most and live a more meaningful life. Recently, she attended a writing conference and took a photo with a well-known author that she would like to
share on her LinkedIn profile.
